# Privacy Policy — Remote for LG TV

Remote for LG TV does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

- The app communicates only with LG TVs on your local Wi-Fi network, directly from your phone to the TV.
- TV pairing keys and addresses are stored only on your device (in the iOS Keychain) and are never sent anywhere except to your own TV.
- The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party services.
- No account is required and no data leaves your local network.

If you have questions, open an issue at https://github.com/benjaminsanborn/lgremote/issues.
